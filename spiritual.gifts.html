Spiritual Gifts Assessment

Based primarily on the Wagner “Modified Houts Questionnaire”, this spiritual gifts discovery instrument was originally suggested by Dr. Richard F. Houts, a professor at the North American Baptist Theological Seminary, in 1976 (it was further informed by C. Peter Wagner and Jon Thompson). The three or four gifts you score highest in are a starting point for prayer and experimentation. Be open to members of the body of Christ helping you confirm what gifts you have.

STEP 1: Assess

Go through the list of statements in the questionnaire; for each, mark to what extent the statement is true of your life: Fully/Frequently, Somewhat/Sometimes, Not Really/Not Often, or Not At All/Not Ever.

STEP 2: Tally

Add up the questionnaire totals by means of the chart provided.

STEP 3: Review/Reflect

Spend time reading the passages where your highest scoring gifts are seen in the Bible. Complete the Reflection questions to gain a tentative evaluation of your gifts and explore the implications for your ministry.
